Interested in Starting a Registry in your State?

The Watershed Resources Registry (WRR) is a transferrable framework. States can establish their own Watershed Resources Registry and be hosted on this website. For more information, contact Ralph Spagnolo at either Spagnolo.ralph@epa.gov or at (215) 814-2718.

The WRR consists of 3 essential components:

location_on

Identify candidate locations

swap_horiz

Assess and compare potential projects

assignment

Export data and print site maps for field visits


Additional information and contact information for specific states’ Watershed Resources Registry can be found by clicking on a state.
